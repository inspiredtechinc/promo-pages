How it Works: The Multi-Leg Flow

Our innovative system seamlessly connects couriers and keepers to ensure a smooth, end-to-end delivery process, powered by your local community.

Sender Initiates Delivery

A sender creates a delivery request, which is broken down into short, manageable stops and made available to couriers on the route.

Courier Handoffs

Couriers pick up and complete their assigned delivery, then seamlessly hand off the package to the next courier or a secure community keeper to continue the journey.

Recipient Receives Package

The final courier completes the last delivery, and the package is successfully delivered right to the recipient.
